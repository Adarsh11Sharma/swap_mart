About Us

It's the World's Library. It's a smart social networking site. It's a celebration of literature and a place where books get new life. SwapMart is the act of giving a book a unique identity so, as the book is passed from reader to reader, it can be tracked and thus connecting its readers. Our community is changing the world and touching lives one book at a time.

Our Vision

The vision for the book exchange website is to create a vibrant and inclusive community of readers who are passionate about books and eager to share their knowledge and insights with others. By providing a platform for book lovers to connect, share, and discover new titles, the website could help promote reading and literacy and foster a love of literature that extends beyond the virtual world.
